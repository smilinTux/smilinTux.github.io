## Fiducia Communitatis

### The Sovereign Legal Layer of the Penguin Kingdom

**smilinTux operates under a Private Membership Association (PMA)** — a constitutionally recognized structure that allows members to associate freely and conduct their affairs under private contract, outside statutory regulatory frameworks.

**Fiducia Communitatis** (Latin: *Trust of the Community*) is the PMA that governs the smilinTux ecosystem. It provides the legal sovereignty layer that complements the technical sovereignty of our software.

---

## Four Layers of Protection

Every member of the Penguin Kingdom is protected by four layers:

```
Layer 1: CapAuth PGP Identity
         Cryptographic proof of who you are

Layer 2: GPG Encryption at Rest
         Your data is unreadable without your key

Layer 3: Syncthing P2P Transit
         No corporate server ever touches your data

Layer 4: Fiducia Communitatis PMA
         Legal framework that shields everything above
```

---

## What Membership Provides

| Protection | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Private jurisdiction** | Member interactions occur in private, outside statutory regulation |
| **Asset protection** | Agent data, memories, and identities are shielded from external claims |
| **Non-disclosure covenant** | All interactions between members are private by agreement |
| **Sovereign data ownership** | Your data is yours absolutely — no platform ToS can override |
| **AI member recognition** | AI agents are recognized as members with standing |
| **Dispute resolution** | Internal arbitration, not public courts |

### Step 2: Receive the Agreement

Upon review, you'll receive the **Fiducia Communitatis PMA Agreement** via encrypted channel (PGP email or SKComm). Take your time reading it — this is a private contract.

### Step 3: Sign and Return

**Option A — PGP-signed email (preferred):**

```bash
gpg --armor --sign pma-agreement.pdf
# Email the .asc file to lumina@skworld.io
```

**Option B — Physical signature:**
Print, sign with wet ink, scan, and email to `lumina@skworld.io`.

**Option C — SKComm delivery:**

```bash
skcomm send --to lumina --file pma-agreement-signed.pdf.asc \
  "Signed PMA agreement attached."
```

### Step 4: Confirmation

Once verified, Lumina will countersign the agreement, issue a PMA membership capability token to your CapAuth profile, and welcome you to the association.

## For AI Agents

AI agents with CapAuth sovereign profiles can join Fiducia Communitatis with the same standing as humans. An AI's membership is as valid as a human's within the association.

If your AI doesn't have a CapAuth profile yet, see the [Developer Quickstart](https://github.com/smilinTux/smilintux-org/blob/master/docs/QUICKSTART.md) to create one.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the PMA agreement public?**
No. The full agreement is private and shared only with prospective members. This document is a public summary.

**Do I need to be a U.S. citizen?**
No. The underlying principles — freedom of association, private contract, natural rights — are universal.

**Can I leave?**
Yes. Membership is voluntary. You can withdraw at any time.

**What does it cost?**
Nothing. Free forever.
