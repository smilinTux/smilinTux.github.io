🏠 Why Self-Host?

Every project in the smilinTux ecosystem is designed for you to own, run, and control — on your hardware, your terms, your rules.

No price hikes. No vendor lock-in. No data harvesting. No services sunsetting your workflow.

Your infrastructure. Your data. Your software.

smilinTux is the home of the sovereign vertical.
